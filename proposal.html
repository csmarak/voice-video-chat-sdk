The Opportunity

HimGra's strongest claim is traceability.
Let's make it technically unassailable.

HimGra positions do actually prove as a core differentiator for luxury brands navigating compliance requirements. But traceability is only as strong as the data infrastructure behind it. Luxury partners and B2B buyers increasingly require digital proof, not just claims.

I want to spend this internship building the backend systems, data pipelines, and AI tooling that give HimGra's traceability story hard technical teeth: queryable supply chain data, intelligent partner-facing tools, and systems that scale with the brand. The page has been reformatted to HimGra's design via AI, but the content is handwritten.

Proposed Work : Four Pillars
01
Supply Chain Traceability API
A Go or FastAPI backend exposing batch origin, fibre grades, and processing steps as structured, queryable data. Suitable for integration with partner portals or compliance reporting tools (EUDR, sustainability audits).
02
RAG-Powered Material Intelligence
A retrieval-augmented system over HimGra's technical specs, certifications, and research docs; basically RAG for HimGra, enabling instant, accurate responses for brand partners, buyers, and internal queries via a simple interface.
03
Partner & Buyer Dashboard
A lightweight web dashboard for B2B partners to access material specs, sample requests, and provenance certificates in one place; reducing friction in the sales and compliance cycle.
04
Geospatial Origin Visualisation
Using PostGIS and mapping tools, build interactive visual proof of Himalayan origin — harvest regions, cultivation zones, processing nodes as a trust-building asset for brand decks and the website.
Concrete Deliverables
  • A production-deployed traceability API (Railway/Vercel) with documented endpoints and at least one working integration
  • A RAG pipeline over HimGra's existing material and certification documents, with a query interface
  • Interactive geospatial map of HimGra's supply chain, exportable for use in brand presentations
  • A lightweight partner portal prototype with material specs, provenance data, and sample request flow
  • Full source code, documentation, and handoff notes for continued development after the internship. These are all ideas pitched as required by the reply email, any similar to suitable projects, I can work on them.
